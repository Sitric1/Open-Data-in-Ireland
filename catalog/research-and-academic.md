# Research & Academic

University and research-lab datasets, reproducible data repositories, and data papers with Ireland focus.

---

### UCD Aerial LiDAR Dublin 2007
- **URL:** https://digital.ucd.ie/view/ucdlib:30462
- **Coverage:** Local (Dublin)
- **Type:** Dataset
- **Theme:** 3D / LiDAR
- **Licence:** Academic (verify at UCD Digital Library)
- **Format:** Point cloud (LAS/LAZ), intensity + colour
- **Access notes:** ~225 million points over ~1 km² of dense urban Dublin; link intermittently unavailable — also listed on watchlist
- **Status:** legacy
- **Description:** High-density aerial laser scanning dataset of Dublin city centre collected in 2007 with intensity and colour information.

---

### NYU 2015 Dublin Aerial LiDAR and Photogrammetry Survey
- **URL:** https://geo.nyu.edu/catalog/nyu_2451_38684
- **Coverage:** Local (Dublin)
- **Type:** Dataset
- **Theme:** 3D / LiDAR / Photogrammetry
- **Licence:** Academic (NYU Spatial Data Repository terms)
- **Format:** LAZ, LAS, PulseWave, RGBi ortho-rasters, oblique imagery
- **Access notes:** 2 km² at 300m altitude; 41 flight paths; link intermittently unavailable — also on watchlist
- **Status:** legacy
- **Description:** Multi-modal aerial sensing dataset of Dublin including dense point cloud, full waveform LiDAR, and ortho-imagery from 2015.

### V-Sense Annotated Dublin City Point Cloud
- **URL:** https://v-sense.scss.tcd.ie/dublincity/
- **Coverage:** Local (Dublin)
- **Type:** Dataset
- **Theme:** 3D / Machine Learning
- **Licence:** Academic (Trinity College Dublin)
- **Format:** Labelled LAS/LAZ (13 semantic classes)
- **Access notes:** Ground truth for ML urban scene understanding; derived from NYU 2015 survey data
- **Status:** active
- **Description:** Manually labelled 3D point cloud of Dublin with 13 semantic classes for machine learning and city-scale reconstruction research.
