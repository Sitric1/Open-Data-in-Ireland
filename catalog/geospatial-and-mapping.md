### OSi Open Data

- **Type:** Portal
- **Theme:** Geospatial
- **Licence:** Creative Commons Attribution 4.0
- **Format:** Shapefile, GeoJSON, KML, REST services

### Irish OpenStreetMap Community
- **URL:** https://www.openstreetmap.ie/
- **Coverage:** All-island
- **Type:** Project
- **Theme:** Geospatial
- **Licence:** ODbL (OpenStreetMap data)
- **Format:** OSM XML/PBF, vector tiles, APIs via openstreetmap.org
- **Access notes:** Community-maintained; sign up at openstreetmap.org to contribute
- **Status:** active
- **Description:** Advocacy and coordination body for OpenStreetMap mapping across the island of Ireland.

### Irish Townlands
- **URL:** https://www.townlands.ie/
- **Coverage:** All-island
- **Type:** Project
- **Theme:** Geospatial / Heritage
- **Licence:** ODbL (derived from OpenStreetMap)
- **Format:** Web interface; underlying OSM data downloadable
- **Access notes:** Crowdsourced mapping of townland, civil parish, and barony boundaries
- **Status:** active
- **Description:** Community initiative mapping Ireland's historic townland boundary system, providing the finest-grained administrative division data available as open data.
