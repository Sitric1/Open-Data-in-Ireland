# Government & Public Bodies

Official publishers (agencies, departments, regulators) offering direct datasets or APIs outside major portals.

---

### Central Statistics Office (CSO)
- **URL:** https://www.cso.ie/en/databases/
- **Coverage:** ROI
- **Type:** Portal
- **Theme:** Statistics / Multi-sector
- **Licence:** Creative Commons Attribution 4.0
- **Format:** CSV, JSON-stat, PX, Linked Data (2011 Census)
- **Access notes:** StatBank API available; also provides Linked Open Data for the 2011 Census
- **Status:** active
- **Description:** Ireland's national statistical office providing demographic, economic, and social data across all major indicators.

---

### Residential Property Price Register
- **URL:** https://www.propertypriceregister.ie/
- **Coverage:** ROI
- **Type:** Dataset
- **Theme:** Property
- **Licence:** Irish PSI General Licence
- **Format:** CSV (downloadable), web search interface
- **Access notes:** Bulk download available; includes address, price, and date of sale since January 2010
- **Status:** active
- **Description:** Register of all residential property purchases in Ireland maintained by the Property Services Regulatory Authority (PSRA).

---

### SEAI National BER Research Tool
- **URL:** https://ndber.seai.ie/BERResearchTool/ber/search.aspx
- **Coverage:** ROI
- **Type:** Dataset
- **Theme:** Energy
- **Licence:** Public access; verify reuse terms at source
- **Format:** Tabular text (downloadable)
- **Access notes:** Search and download interface; building energy rating data for individual dwellings
- **Status:** active
- **Description:** Building Energy Rating (BER) certificate data indicating energy performance for Irish dwellings, administered by SEAI.

---

### Fáilte Ireland
- **URL:** https://www.failteireland.ie/Research-and-Insights.aspx
- **Coverage:** ROI
- **Type:** Dataset
- **Theme:** Tourism
- **Licence:** Verify at source; datasets also available via Data.gov.ie
- **Format:** CSV, PDF reports
- **Access notes:** Developer Portal/API previously available (now inactive); CSV datasets accessible via Data.gov.ie
- **Status:** legacy
- **Description:** National Tourism Development Authority providing research insights and location-based tourism datasets (activities, attractions, accommodation).

---

### HSE TrolleyGAR (Patients Awaiting Admission)
- **URL:** https://www.hse.ie/eng/services/campaigns/trolleygar.html
- **Coverage:** ROI
- **Type:** Dataset
- **Theme:** Health
- **Licence:** Unclear
- **Format:** Web interface
- **Access notes:** Historically reported 3x daily (8am, 2pm, 8pm); link currently inactive
- **Status:** legacy
- **Description:** Hospital emergency department trolley counts from HSE acute hospitals, tracking patients awaiting inpatient bed admission.

---

### INMO Trolley Watch
- **URL:** https://www.inmo.ie/Trolley_Ward_Watch
- **Coverage:** ROI
- **Type:** Dataset
- **Theme:** Health
- **Licence:** Unclear
- **Format:** Web interface
- **Access notes:** Daily count at 8am by INMO members; link currently inactive
- **Status:** legacy
- **Description:** Daily count of patients on trolleys in emergency departments and wards, collected by the Irish Nurses and Midwives Organisation since 2004.

---

### Companies Registration Office (CRO)
- **URL:** https://www.cro.ie/
- **Coverage:** ROI
- **Type:** API
- **Theme:** Business
- **Licence:** Public access; verify commercial reuse terms
- **Format:** RESTful JSON (CRO Open Services)
- **Access notes:** API at https://services.cro.ie/ returns company names, addresses, Eircodes, registration dates and statuses
- **Status:** active
- **Description:** Central repository of statutory information on Irish companies and business names, with a public search API.
