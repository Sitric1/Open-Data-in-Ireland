# Cities & Regions

City and county data portals, local authority open-data initiatives, and regional datasets.

---

## Dublin

### Smart Dublin (Dublinked)
- **URL:** https://data.smartdublin.ie/
- **Coverage:** Local (Dublin region)
- **Type:** Portal
- **Theme:** Multi-sector
- **Licence:** Mixed (dataset-specific)
- **Format:** CSV, JSON, GeoJSON, APIs (CKAN-based)
- **Access notes:** Operated by the four Dublin Local Authorities as part of the Smart Dublin initiative
- **Status:** active
- **Description:** Open data portal for the Dublin region providing datasets across transport, environment, planning, and civic services.

---

### Dublin Economic Monitor
- **URL:** https://data.smartdublin.ie/dataset/dublin-economic-monitor
- **Coverage:** Local (Dublin)
- **Type:** Dataset
- **Theme:** Economy
- **Licence:** Verify at source (Smart Dublin terms)
- **Format:** CSV, CKAN API
- **Access notes:** 13 regional economic indicators for the Dublin City Region
- **Status:** active
- **Description:** Economic development indicators for the Dublin region including employment, enterprise, and housing metrics.

## Cork

### Cork Smart Gateway
- **URL:** http://data.corkcity.ie/
- **Coverage:** Local (Cork)
- **Type:** Portal
- **Theme:** Multi-sector
- **Licence:** Mixed (dataset-specific)
- **Format:** CSV, JSON, GeoJSON
- **Access notes:** Open format data for download, research, application development, and visualisation
- **Status:** active
- **Description:** Cork city's open data portal providing datasets to support research, civic apps, and transparency.
