# Heritage & Culture

Digital heritage collections, monument inventories, cultural archives with structured data access.

---

### National Monuments Service — Historic Environment Viewer
- **URL:** https://maps.archaeology.ie/HistoricEnvironment/
- **Coverage:** ROI
- **Type:** Viewer
- **Theme:** Heritage
- **Licence:** Verify at source (Department of Housing, Local Government and Heritage)
- **Format:** Web map viewer, WMS
- **Access notes:** Archaeological survey data, recorded monuments, and protection zones; some 3D content for selected sites
- **Status:** active
- **Description:** Interactive map of Ireland's recorded archaeological monuments, historic buildings, and protected structures maintained by the National Monuments Service.

---

### National Inventory of Architectural Heritage (NIAH)
- **URL:** https://www.buildingsofireland.ie/
- **Coverage:** ROI
- **Type:** Dataset
- **Theme:** Heritage
- **Licence:** Verify at source (DHLGH)
- **Format:** Web search, structured records with images
- **Access notes:** Searchable database of structures of architectural heritage merit; individual records with descriptions, photos, and ratings
- **Status:** active
- **Description:** Systematic record of Ireland's architectural heritage used to advise planning authorities on protection of historic buildings.

---

### Dúchas — National Folklore Collection
- **URL:** https://www.duchas.ie/
- **Coverage:** All-island
- **Type:** Archive
- **Theme:** Heritage / Culture
- **Licence:** Creative Commons (varies by collection)
- **Format:** Digitized manuscripts, structured metadata, search interface
- **Access notes:** Schools' Collection, Main Manuscript Collection, and Photographic Collection; crowdsourced transcription ongoing
- **Status:** active
- **Description:** Digitized National Folklore Collection of Ireland providing access to oral history, folklore manuscripts, and associated photographs.

---

### Logainm — Placenames Database of Ireland
- **URL:** https://www.logainm.ie/
- **Coverage:** All-island
- **Type:** Dataset
- **Theme:** Heritage / Geospatial
- **Licence:** Creative Commons Attribution 4.0
- **Format:** Linked Data, JSON API, web interface
- **Access notes:** Bilingual Irish/English placename data with coordinates; API documentation at docs.logainm.ie
- **Status:** active
- **Description:** Authoritative bilingual database of Irish placenames with geographic coordinates, maintained by Fiontar & Scoil na Gaeilge (DCU) and the Placenames Branch.

---

### CELT — Corpus of Electronic Texts
- **URL:** https://celt.ucc.ie/
- **Coverage:** All-island
- **Type:** Archive
- **Theme:** Culture / Research
- **Licence:** Open access (academic; verify per-text terms)
- **Format:** TEI XML, HTML
- **Access notes:** Hosted by University College Cork; historical, literary, and legal texts in Irish, English, Latin, and French
- **Status:** active
- **Description:** Online resource of Irish historical and literary texts in multiple languages, encoded in TEI XML for scholarly use.

---

### Digital Repository of Ireland (DRI)
- **URL:** https://www.dri.ie/
- **Coverage:** All-island
- **Type:** Archive
- **Theme:** Heritage / Culture / Research
- **Licence:** Mixed (per-collection; many CC licences)
- **Format:** Metadata (Dublin Core, MODS), digital objects (images, audio, documents)
- **Access notes:** National trusted digital repository; collections from libraries, museums, archives, and galleries
- **Status:** active
- **Description:** Ireland's national digital preservation repository aggregating cultural heritage and research collections with structured metadata access.

---

### Irish Townlands (Heritage context)
- **URL:** https://www.townlands.ie/
- **Coverage:** All-island
- **Type:** Project
- **Theme:** Heritage / Geospatial
- **Licence:** ODbL (OpenStreetMap-derived)
- **Format:** Web interface, OSM data
- **Access notes:** Cross-listed with Geospatial & Mapping; pre-Norman administrative boundary system
- **Status:** active
- **Description:** Crowdsourced mapping of Ireland's ancient townland boundary system providing fine-grained historical administrative geography.
