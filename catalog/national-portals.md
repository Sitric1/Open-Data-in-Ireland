# National Portals

State-scale discovery platforms and umbrella catalogues for Ireland-related open data.

---

### Data.gov.ie
- **URL:** https://data.gov.ie/
- **Coverage:** ROI
- **Type:** Portal
- **Theme:** Multi-sector
- **Licence:** Mixed (dataset-specific; majority use Irish PSI General Licence or CC BY 4.0)
- **Format:** CSV, JSON, XML, Geo formats (varies by publisher)
- **Access notes:** Central discovery portal; quality and freshness vary by dataset owner
- **Status:** active
- **Description:** Primary Republic of Ireland portal for public-sector open data, hosting thousands of datasets across all government departments and agencies.

---

### OpenDataNI
- **URL:** https://www.opendatani.gov.uk/
- **Coverage:** NI
- **Type:** Portal
- **Theme:** Multi-sector
- **Licence:** UK Open Government Licence (OGL)
- **Format:** CSV, JSON, XML, GeoJSON (varies by publisher)
- **Access notes:** Developed by the Open Data Team as part of the Digital NI initiative
- **Status:** active
- **Description:** Northern Ireland's official open data portal providing access to non-sensitive public sector data for reuse and redistribution.

---

### EU Open Data Portal
- **URL:** https://data.europa.eu/
- **Coverage:** All-island (as part of EU/EEA datasets)
- **Type:** Portal
- **Theme:** Multi-sector
- **Licence:** Mixed (dataset-specific; many CC BY or open equivalent)
- **Format:** CSV, JSON, RDF, SPARQL endpoints
- **Access notes:** Includes Ireland-specific subsets; search by country filter
- **Status:** active
- **Description:** European Union open data portal with Ireland-relevant datasets from EU institutions and cross-border programmes.
